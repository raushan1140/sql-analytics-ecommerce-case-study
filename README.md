## 🎯 Quick Summary

This project analyzes the **Brazilian Olist E-Commerce dataset** with **15 SQL analytical queries** to answer key business questions about returns, profitability, and customer behavior.

**Key Finding:** 🚨 **0% repeat customer retention rate** - all customers are one-time buyers (critical business issue)

## 🎯 Key Findings Summary

### Critical Issues
- **0% Customer Retention** - Every customer is one-time buyer
- **Geographic Concentration** - 15% of customers in São Paulo only
- **High Freight Burden** - Northeast regions pay 17.26% of order value in freight

### Strengths
- Exceptional on-time delivery performance
- High customer satisfaction when delivered on time
- Health & Beauty category performing strongly

### Opportunities
- Northeast market expansion (+89% higher AOV)
- Retention program could add $32M+ annually
- Break growth plateau at 7K orders/month

---

## 📊 Strategic Recommendations

**Immediate (0-3 months):**
1. Launch retention program (target: 5% repeat rate)
2. Quality assurance for high-volume categories
3. Optimize Northeast/North freight costs

**Medium-term (3-6 months):**
4. Geographic expansion to Northeast cities
5. New growth initiatives to break plateau
6. Seller network diversification

**Long-term (6-12 months):**
7. Move customer lifetime value from $160 → $500+
